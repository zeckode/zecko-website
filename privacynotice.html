What personal information about customers does Zecko collect?

We collect your personal information in order to provide and continually improve our products and services.

Here are the types of personal information we collect:
We receive and store any information you provide in relation to Zecko Services. You can choose not to provide certain information, but then you might not be able to take advantage of many of our Services. This information includes, but is not limited to contact details for the purpose of sharing updates about your orders, updates to your account and address details shared with our sellers to initiate product delivery.

We automatically collect and store certain types of information about your use of Zecko Services, including information about your interaction with content and services available through Zecko Services. Like many websites, we use cookies and other unique identifiers, and we obtain certain types of information when your web browser or device accesses Zecko Services and other content served by or on behalf of Zecko on other websites.

We might receive information about you from other sources, such as updated delivery and address information from our carriers, which we use to correct our records and deliver your next purchase more easily.

How does Zecko use your information?

Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems, and are required to keep the information confidential. In addition, all sensitive/credit information you supply is encrypted by our us and our payment partners. We use your personal information to operate, provide, develop, and improve the products and services that we offer our customers. These purposes include:

  • Purchase and delivery of products and services.
    • We and our affiliates use your personal information to take and fulfil orders, deliver products and services, process payments, and communicate with you about orders, products and services, and promotional offers.
  • Provide, troubleshoot, and improve Zecko Services.
    • We and our affiliates use your personal information to provide functionality, analyse performance, fix errors, and improve the usability and effectiveness of the Zecko Services.
  • Recommendations and personalization.
    • We and our affiliates use your personal information to recommend features, products, and services that might be of interest to you, identify your preferences, and personalize your experience with Zecko Services.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
    • In certain cases, we and our affiliates collect and use your personal information to comply with laws. For instance, from sellers we collect information regarding place of establishment and bank account information for identity verification and other purposes.

  • We and our affiliates use your personal information to display interest-based ads for features, products, and services that might be of interest to you. We do not use information that personally identifies you to display interest-based ads.

  • Conducting research and diagnostics to improve Zecko's content, products, and services.
  • Preventing fraudulent activity.
  • Improving security.
  • Delivering content, including ads, relevant to your interests across our partner websites, apps and other platforms.
  • This allows us to measure and analyse the performance of our services.
    • Zecko's cookies allow you to take advantage of some of Zecko's essential features. For instance, if you block or otherwise reject our cookies, you may not be able to add items to your Shopping Cart, proceed to Checkout, or use any Zecko products and services that require you to sign in.
